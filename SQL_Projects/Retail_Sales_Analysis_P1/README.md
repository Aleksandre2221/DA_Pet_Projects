## Description
This project performs **data cleaning, exploration, and business-oriented SQL analysis** on a retail sales dataset.  
The objective is to understand customer behavior, sales performance, and product profitability through SQL queries.

## Case Study Questions
The analysis answers key business questions, including:

- Sales on specific dates  
- High-value transactions and top customers  
- Sales and profit analysis by category  
- Monthly trends and best-selling periods  
- Weekday and shift-based sales performance  
- Customer purchase diversity (multi-category buyers)  
- Customers spending above global averages  

## Findings
- **Category Performance:** Clothing and Electronics generated the highest revenue.  
- **Customer Behavior:** A small percentage of customers contributed disproportionately to total sales.  
- **Sales Trends:** Evenings and specific weekdays show increased activity.  
- **Profitability:** Profit margins vary widely across categories, highlighting key strategic areas.  
- **Demographics:** Beauty category buyers tend to be younger on average.
